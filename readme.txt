# 🎣 Angln Academy

Youth Fishing Training & Development Platform  
Greenwood, South Carolina → Greater Greenville Area

---

## 🌱 Mission

To develop confident anglers while instilling the values of respect, integrity, and responsibility through structured outdoor education.

---

## 🎯 Overview

Angln Academy is a youth-focused fishing training program designed to guide anglers from their first cast to competitive readiness through structured, hands-on learning.

This repository contains the website and digital foundation supporting a real-world fishing academy offering:

- Beginner fishing clinics  
- Parent + child training sessions  
- Youth development programs  
- Competitive fishing preparation  
- Community-driven outdoor education  

---

## 🧩 Core Features

- 🗓 Event listings (clinics, camps, training sessions)  
- 📝 Online registration and sign-up flows  
- 💳 Pricing tiers and program breakdowns  
- 👨‍👩‍👧 Parent + child program visibility  
- 🎣 Beginner → advanced training pathways  
- 📍 Location-based offerings (Greenwood / Greenville)  

---

## 🏗 Business Model

Angln Academy operates as a scalable service-based business with multiple revenue streams:

- Group clinics  
- Private coaching  
- Monthly memberships  
- Starter fishing kits  
- Seasonal camps and tournaments  

---
